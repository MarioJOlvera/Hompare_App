In this section, it is possible to make comparisons
between states and municipalities. Detailed data, that was considered
important to people when buying a home, is displayed. Besides price,
it was decided to show some of the most common indicators that may influence people´s decisions when they are searching for a house.

Below you can find:

  • Average price
  • Properties offered
  • Average total surface price per square meter
  • Average built surface price per square meter
  • The average number of bedrooms
  • Health, education, income and Human Development indicators
